Spain

Art

Discovering Spain means traveling across time and being astounded by an abundance of structures, from the most avant-garde, futuristic architecture to medieval castles and an ancient Roman aqueduct, is what it means to discover Spain. Spain has some of the most incredible artistic legacies in the world, a result of centuries of blending cultures. You will be mesmerized by the histories behind each piece of art when you visit us and will never forget them.Here you'll find some of the most exquisite works of art by Velázquez, Picasso, Dalí, and Gaudí. It's also worth exploring them on one of the original guided tours available, following in the footsteps of the greatest artists at museums like the Prado, or spending a moonlit evening at Granada's Alhambra.

Local Cruisine

Enjoy the greatest way to discover Spain—while eating! Apart from the numerous customary tapas, there's Valencian paella, regional stews, hams, and sausages, as well as delectable desserts. Delicious meat dishes and fish that are best enjoyed by the sea may be found in Spain's incredibly diverse cuisine. Products from southern vegetable gardens and the superb simplicity of northern cuisine enhance traditional inland recipes. Spain's culinary traditions, exemplified by the Mediterranean diet, are both delicious and healthful. Don't hesitate any longer and be ready to savor the finest Spanish cuisine.

Culture

Spain boasts a friendly and lively culture. The populace is ardent, expressive, and affectionate. Family is their top priority, and they schedule time to spend with their loved ones, especially over meals. They take things slowly because they want to appreciate life to the fullest. When it comes to enjoying customs, they don't hold back and take great pride in their artistic, historical, and cultural history.
