Florence,Italy

Museums

The city has been making art lovers of us all for centuries. Palazzo Vecchio is a must see palace that should be at the top of your list. This museum even though it has great history and design many civilians know it for the replica of Michelangelo's David.

Food

Food is one of the keystones of Italian culture and even when life becomes busy Italians still find a great pleasure in sitting at their dining table or at a restraunt. Which is counter to American fast food culture. We do not have time with our fast pace lives to sit down and enjoy a meal. Maybe this is why Italians are happier with life that Americans.

Architecture

The Italian architectural style is considered one of the classic styles. This style is characterized by its use of arches and columns. Many buildings in italy are characterized by features such as domes and columns. During the Renaissance, artists hoped to create structures that would appeal to both emotion and reason. Three key figures in Renaissance architecture were Filippo Brunelleschi, Leon Battista Alberti, and Andrea Palladio.
