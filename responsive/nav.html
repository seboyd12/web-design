Sightseeing Tours

Come check out the beautiful city of florernce on the top of our open top,two story sightseeing buses. They have only been up and running in Florence for a few years but they are a perfect way to see the city. The buses follow a route to take you by all the main sights as well as the sights that are a hidden gem. These hidden gems might be easily missed if you are going through the city quickly in a car or by foot. Book now with us to add another adventure on your trip!

Nearby Day Trips

If you are interested in seeing areas outside of Florence by bus we are happy to help you plan that day trip! All of the main ciites have their own bus company and they run all the buses across the region. We even offer to make lunch reservations along the way so that you can get a taste of different cuisine along the way. Everyone on social media will be so jealous of your food blog along the way. We also help set up vineyard tours if you are interested in checking that out. We hope you consider booking a day trip with us.
